Beans on Toast

Description

The following recipe is for the well known British classic, Beans on Toast. This quick meal can be eaten at all time of the day and will cost less than 50p per portion.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of white bread
  • 1 tin of heinz beans
  • 40g of grated mature cheddar

Method

  1. Toast the slices of bread
  2. Warm beans on the hob for 3-5 minutes and stir constantly
  3. Grate cheese.
  4. Place toast on plate. Pour on beans.
  5. Sprinkle on cheese according to taste
